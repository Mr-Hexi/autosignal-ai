Indian Automobile Sector Intelligence Report (18 March 2026)

The Indian automobile sector's overall sentiment remains neutral at 5.4/10, indicating a mixed market sentiment. Despite this, the sector's average sentiment has been trending upwards, suggesting improving market conditions. However, the sector's average RSI (Relative Strength Index) of 36.2 indicates that the sector is still in a bearish trend, with 4 out of 5 companies trading below their 20-day moving averages (MA20). The sector's average revenue growth of 24.1% and average profitability score of 6.4/10 indicate that companies are still navigating challenges, but with some signs of resilience.

Maruti Suzuki also lags behind with a sentiment score of 5.06/10, despite its high revenue growth of 28.7%, due to its low profit margin of 8.7% and bearish RSI of 31.6.

Investors should exercise caution when considering investments in the sector, given the bearish trend and low RSI levels.
